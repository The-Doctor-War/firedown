Quality sheet + grid item — consistency redesign

Two problems in the current build: (1) the Download-quality sheet mixes big filled video+audio pills + radios with a bolted-on captions block in a different visual language; (2) the grid tile scatters a CC chip top-left and a mime/duration/quality cluster bottom, in two different chip styles. Proposals below unify each into one coherent token system.

1 · Download sheet — one picker, two sections, one token language

Kill the loud filled video+audio pill on every row — it repeats the same word six times and competes with the selection control. Replace with a quiet resolution-led row: title line = resolution + a small quality tag, meta line = size · codec. Captions reuse the exact same row rhythm (checkbox instead of radio, a lang chip instead of a quality tag). Shared uppercase section headers tie them together. CTA tallies the combined selection.
